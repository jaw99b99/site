My Approach

What is this competency?

In BUT MMI, the "Understand" competency is the foundation of every digital project. Before designing a website or a video, it is essential to analyse the environment, understand user needs, and decrypt existing communication strategies. A beautiful design is useless if it doesn't connect with the right audience!

The Project: Iron Maiden vs SOAD Benchmark

To put this competency into practice, I carried out a detailed comparative benchmark between the websites of two iconic metal bands: Iron Maiden and System of a Down. The objective was to analyse how each band adapts its communication and web ergonomics to its fan community.

Key Analysis Steps

  • Ergonomic & Visual Audit
    Comparison of the UI and UX of the two sites: navigation, clarity, visual identity.
  • Communication Strategy
    Study of the tone and content prioritisation (shops, tour dates, news).
  • Persona Creation
    Development of realistic user profiles to precisely define the age, digital habits, and expectations of each site's typical visitor.

What I Learned

This analytical work taught me to always put myself in the end user's shoes before starting the creation phase. Understanding the target audience is the key to effective digital communication.
