My Approach

The Project: Blois Tourist Office Redesign

As part of a group of 4, our mission was to draw up the full scoping document (note de cadrage) for a complete redesign of the Blois-Chambord Tourist Office website. For a project estimated at 6–9 months with a maximum budget of €100,000, I participated in drafting the financial plan.

Budget Breakdown (~€65,800 total)

  • Hardware (~€4,300)
    Purchase of 4 complete workstations (computers, dual screens, peripherals).
  • Software & Infrastructure
    Free solutions (WordPress, VSCode) + OVH hosting at €100–300/month for high-traffic site.
  • Labour (~€30,600)
    Hourly cost for 4 junior profiles over 9 months (~2h/day/person), including charges.

Business Finance Concepts

Financial viability is at the heart of every project. Through various practical exercises, I applied key business management concepts:

  • Cost of production calculation
  • Psychological pricing
  • Break-even analysis

What I Learned

Knowing how to manage a project also means knowing how to communicate clearly and visually. I created an infographic summarising data on the use of the French language worldwide — transforming raw data into a striking communication tool. Entrepreneurship is about turning vision into reality, step by step.
